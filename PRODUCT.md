# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users
Technical Recruiters at top-tier tech companies, Engineering Managers looking for Android specialists, and potential freelance clients. They are looking for high-level expertise, reliability, and leadership in mobile engineering.

## Product Purpose
Establish Mohamed Wael as a top-tier Senior\Lead Android Engineer and App Architect. The site serves as a professional portfolio to showcase high-impact global applications, increase interview invites, and attract high-value freelance opportunities by demonstrating technical mastery and leadership.

## Positioning
The Senior Android Engineer who builds the architectures and features powering global apps like Vodafone and WeightWatchers.

## Conversion & proof
- Primary and secondary CTA: Email me, Connect on LinkedIn, Download PDF CV.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "Building the core Android experiences for global giants."
- Belief ladder:
    1. He has deep technical mastery of Android and modern toolsets.
    2. He can lead and scale complex features and engineering teams.
    3. He is a high-value collaborator who delivers production-grade results.
- Proof on hand: Detailed career history at WeightWatchers, Careem, and Vodafone; Play Store links for global apps; implementation of unified app frameworks (MVA10).

## Brand Personality
Technical, Sleek, Innovative, Minimalist. The vibe should be "Expert Confidence"—clean and precise without unnecessary flair.

## Anti-references
- Generic Bootstrap templates or "cookie-cutter" resume sites.
- Overly flashy or distracting animations that detract from content.
- Cluttered layouts or "loud" color schemes.

## Design Principles
- **Expert Precision**: Every detail (spacing, typography, alignment) should feel intentional and calibrated, mirroring the precision required in SDK development.
- **Content-First Minimalism**: The technical achievements are the star. Use whitespace and hierarchy to make the content effortless to consume.
- **Subtle Innovation**: Use modern web techniques (smooth transitions, refined typography) to signal "Technical/Innovative" without being "Flashy".

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability.
- Screen-reader friendly semantic markup.
- Support for reduced motion and keyboard navigation.
- **Dark Mode Support**: System-preference-aware dark theme.
- **Localization**: Support for multiple languages (English/Arabic).
